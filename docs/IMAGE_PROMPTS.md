# Prop turnaround prompts (4 orthographic views)

Fiecare bloc de mai jos e self-contained — dai unul la GPT, primești 4 imagini (front, back, left side, right side) folosibile ca referință pentru modelare 3D.

## Master directives (incluse implicit în fiecare prompt de mai jos)

```text
STYLE LOCK — read carefully and apply to every image:
Stylized low-poly 3D game asset with hand-painted textures AND flat-shading normals (every polygon face is a distinct planar surface with crisp visible hard edges between polygons — NOT smooth-shaded, NOT auto-smooth normals, NOT subdivision surface, NOT rounded blended geometry). Reduced polygon count (target 800–1500 triangles per asset — chunky, deliberately low). Simplified faceted geometry — readable silhouette, clearly visible chunky polygon facets across all curved surfaces, larger polygon planes (not tiny micro-facets). Hand-painted texture maps with VERY FAINT, barely perceptible aged grime and a light hint of dust baked into the texture — minimal restraint, just enough to avoid clean-new-asset look, NOT visibly dirty, NOT mud-caked, NOT heavy weathering. Textures stay predominantly clean with a soft atmospheric tint. NOT modeled as displacement. Painterly material treatment — earthy, muted, atmospheric. Soft ambient occlusion and gentle baked lighting allowed in the texture. Reference look: Sable game (Shedworks) flat-shaded faceted hand-painted style, A Plague Tale Innocence prop style with VISIBLE polygons, Death Stranding occasional faceted low-poly props, Hellblade stylized low-poly. AVOID: smooth Phong shading, subdivision modifier output, ZBrush sculpts, marvelous designer cloth, photoreal PBR with normal maps that hide polygon facets, hyper-real skin pores, fabric weave macro detail, displacement-mapped wood grain, 8K subdivision modeling, Cycles photorealistic render. AVOID: Synty POLYGON flat cel-shaded cartoon look, Pixar smooth cartoon, anime chibi proportions, Roblox blocky, Sky Children of the Light pastel. Target: stylized but grounded, dusty and worn, hand-painted textures, AND with clearly visible flat-shaded polygon facets across the model surface.

FRAMING — apply to every image:
Render 4 SEPARATE images of the same asset: 1) front view, 2) back view, 3) left side profile, 4) right side profile. Strict orthographic projection, no perspective distortion. Asset centered, identical scale and proportions across all 4 images. Neutral medium-grey background (#808080), flat even studio lighting, no harsh shadows, no environment, no props. Single asset only. No text overlays, no labels.

WORLD ANCHOR — apply to every image:
Qin Dynasty 210 BC China, ancient sealed tomb stealth game, dark tragic mood. Muted earth-tone palette: warm terracotta red-brown, dull oxidized bronze, dark grey-brown stone, weathered raw wood, dusty beige hemp linen, faded ochre. Aged surfaces with restrained grime baked into texture. Not Japanese, not samurai, not medieval European, not fantasy, not magical, not modern.
```

## How to use

1. Copiezi un singur bloc de cod la GPT (sau direct la modelul de imagini dacă suportă text lung).
2. GPT primește instrucțiunea să genereze 4 imagini orthographice cu același obiect. Dacă GPT refuză să genereze 4 imagini într-un singur turn, cere-i explicit "first the front view, then back, then left, then right".
3. Verifică coerența: proporțiile, culorile, detaliile trebuie să se potrivească pe toate 4 vederile. Dacă diferă, regenerezi una singură cerând "match the proportions of the previous front view".
4. Asseturile rezultate intră în Godot prin folderele:
   - props handheld → `res://scenes/items/` (înlocuiește placeholder-ele din [inventory.gd](Scripts/inventory.gd) și [oil_lamp.tscn](scenes/items/oil_lamp.tscn))
   - props scenice → `res://Models/Props/` (nou)
   - personaje → `res://Models/Characters/` (nou)
5. Toate modelele se importă ca `.glb` cu scale 1.0 (1 unitate = 1 m).
