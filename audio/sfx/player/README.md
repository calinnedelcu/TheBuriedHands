# Player Movement SFX

Drop short real footstep samples here. The player controller loads supported files automatically at runtime.

Recommended folders:

- `footsteps/` - 8 to 12 short dry steps on dusty stone, packed earth, clay, or terracotta floor.
- `jump/` - 2 to 4 short clothing/effort takeoff sounds, no cartoon jump.
- `land/` - 4 to 8 short dusty thumps, with a few heavier impacts.

Supported formats: `.wav`, `.ogg`, `.mp3`.

Suggested naming:

- `stone_dust_step_01.wav`
- `stone_dust_step_02.wav`
- `cloth_jump_01.wav`
- `dust_land_01.wav`

Keep them dry and subtle. Reverb can be handled later through a Godot audio bus for the tomb.
